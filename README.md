## FAQ
 
**Does it actually fix things or just suggest fixes?**
Actually fixes. On WordPress, Webflow, and Shopify we push changes via the official API. Fixes land as drafts by default. Flip a toggle and we auto-ship within rules you set.
 
**Will it break my site?**
Every fix is reversible with one click. A full diff log is kept. Dangerous changes (redirects, `robots.txt`) require explicit approval even on auto-ship. 400k+ fixes shipped. 0.03% ever reverted.
 
**Is this just GPT with extra steps?**
No. The crawler is custom-built. The impact-ranking model is trained on 2 years of rank-correlated fix data. LLMs handle plain-English explanations and content drafts — they don't decide what to fix.
 
**Can I cancel the monthly plan anytime?**
Yes. One click in settings. No email, no exit interview, no "are you sure" three times. Cancel mid-month and you keep access through the period.
